By the Master and Wardens of the Company of Stationers. To all Free-men of the said Company. YOu are required (according to your Oath) to make your appearance at Stationers Hall on Monday the thirtieth day of this present June, at eight of the Clock in the morning, being Quarter day, and a Com∣mon-hall; whereof you are not to fail, and you are desi∣red not to depart untill the Court shall dismisse you. Stationers Hall  the 21th of June, 1645.
