A CATALOGVE OF THE NAMES OF the Knights for the Counties, Citizens, Burgesses for the Boroughes, and Barons for the Ports for the House of Commons, for this PARLIAMENT. BEGVN AT WESTMINSTER the 3. of November, 1640. LONDON, Printed for Tho: Walkley, and are to be sold at his Shop, at the signe of the flying Horse, betweene Brittains Bursse, and Yorke House. 1640.
A Proclamation before the L. STEVVARD. THe Kings most excellent Majesty doth straightly charge and command all manner of Persons chosen for the Knights, Citizens, and Burgesses, to attend in this present Parliament, that they, nor any of them do presume to sit or take their places in the lower House of Parlia∣ment; untill they, and every of them have first taken the Oathes of Supremacie, and Allegiance in the usuall man∣ner and place; nor untill the Sheriffe shall make returne of his Writ according to Statute, unto the Clarke of the Crowne in Chancery, and his, or their names bee there en∣tred in such manner as hath heretofore beene accustomed, upon paine of the perill shall fall thereon. God save the King. ALl Knights, Citizens, and Burgesses, chosen to attend this present Parliament; make your ap∣pearance, and answer to your names as you shall bee called. The names of the Knights, Citizens, and Burgesses of the Counties, Cities, and Borough-Townes of Eng∣land, and Wales, and the Baronie of the Ports now sit∣ting in Parliament holden at Westminster the 3. day of November, 1640. in the sixteenth yeare of the Raigne of our Soveraigne Lord King Charles.
