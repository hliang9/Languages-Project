blazon or coat of arms AN ACT For Continuing Two former Acts touching ELECTIONS IN The City of London. BE it Enacted by this present Parliament, and by the Authority thereof, That one Act, Entituled, An Act disabling the Election of divers persons to any Office or place of Trust within the City of London, and the Votes of such persons in such Ele∣ctions: And also one other Act, Entituled, An Act concerning the Election of Questmen, Con∣stables, and all other subordinate Officers whatsoever, within the City of London and Liberties thereof, be, and hereby are Revived and Continued, and shall be, according to the true scope, intent and meaning of the said several Acts, in all things ob∣served, kept and executed, for the space of One whole year, from the Eighteenth day of December, One thousand six hundred and fifty. Die Mercurii, 18 Decembr. 1650. ORdered by the Parliament, That this Act be forthwith printed and pub∣lished. Hen: Scobell, Cleric. Parliamenti. London, Printed by Edward Husband and Iohn Field, Printers to the Parliament of England, 1650.
