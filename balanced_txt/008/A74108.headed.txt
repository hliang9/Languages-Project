Die Sabbathi, 8. Julii, 1648. ORdered by the Commons assembled in Parliament, That Thankes be given to Almighty God, to mor∣row, the Ninth of this instant July, in all the Churches and Chappels within the late Lines of Communication, for the great Victory it pleased God to give the Par∣liament Forces under the Command of Colonell Edward Rossiter, over the Enemies Forces in Willoughby-fields in the County of Nottingham: And that the Lord Major be desired to give no∣tice hereof to the severall Ministers of those severall Places. H. Elsynge, Cler. Parl. D. Com.
