To JACK BILL, in THANKS For his Christening-Entertainment AT CANE-VVOOD, June 13. 1661. THe various Canes from several Parts are brought; Some for the Eyes; some, for Support are bought: But sure this Wood belonging unto BILL, Are Sugar-Canes, where each might suck his fill Of such high Entertainment, as did raise Content to all, and to the Donor Praise. BASTA.
