His Highness Prince Rupert Governour. Sir George Carteret Deputy-Governour. Sir John Robinson Sir John Griffith Sir James Hayes Sir Edward Hungerford Sir John Kirke John Bence Esq John Lindsay Esq Treasurer. Every Adventurer is to have so many Votes as he has Hundred Pounds in the General Stock. No Person who has adventured less than 200 l is capable of being chosen one of the Committee, whereof only four of the last are to remain, and three others to be chosen out of the Company, and added to them for the Year ensuing.
