OSSORY, WHEREAS the Parliament stands Prorogued untill the first day of December next; And whereas for sundry Causes and Considera∣tions; We have thought it fit and convenient and necessary further to Prorogue the said Parliament untill the Seventeenth day of Ja∣nuary next; We do therefore will and require all Persons to whom it doth or shall appertain to take due notice thereof, to the end that in the mean time they may repair to their Houses or otherwise apply themselves about their private Affairs at their pleasures, And then to give their attendance here again, at the day and time aforesaid, in the usual place ap∣pointed for that intended Service, whereof they may not fail.
