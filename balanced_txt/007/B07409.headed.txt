SONET Frabanc to banc fra vod to vod J rin Ovrhailit vith my feble fantasie Lyc til a leif yat fallis from a trie Or til a reid ovrblavin vith ye vind. Tva gods gyds me ye ane of yam is blind, Ʒe and a bairn brocht vp in uanitie, The nixt a vyf ingenrit of ye se, And lichter nor a davphin vith hir sin. Vnhappie is ye man for euirmaire That teils ye sand and savis in ye aire, Bot tvyse vnhappier is he J lairn Th fedis in his hairt a mad desyre▪ And follovs ou a voman yrov ye fyre Led be a blind and teichit be a bairn M.
