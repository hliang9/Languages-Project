MYSTERIA MEA MIHI IN IEHOVAH PERILA ALTISSIMA VINXI Thy Mottos lyke Thy mind, thats much retird: With which when men of yore were once inspird, They had their Heauen, and thought them selues most pure. Which made them glorious in a life obscure WORLDS glorie is but like the lightnings flame. That soon goes out, as kindled is the same And if yerout it go it ought doth scale Its loftie Cedar not the Shrub in dale The H., content thee with thy state and place If thou seek glorie let it be by grace I.D. Cambro Britannus
