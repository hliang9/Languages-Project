WHereas I Roger Altham did License a Book, Entitu∣led A Vindication of the Primitive Fathers, &c. writ∣ten by Mr. Samuel Hill of Killington, in the Diocess of Bath and Wells, &c. in which are contained many Scur∣rilous Reflections upon, and malicious Insinuations of Heresie, and secret Practise against the R.
