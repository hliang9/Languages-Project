EXTRAORDINARY NEWES FROM Colonell IOHN BARKEER Governour of Coventry, TO A MERCHANT OF LONDON. Shewing how Sir WILLIAM BRERETON hath raised the siege from Namptwich in Cheshire. SIR, I Have sent you the good News which we have in our parts. The Messenger being in haste I could not write any larger to you. Iohn Barker. Ianuar. 30. 1643.
