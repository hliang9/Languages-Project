The METHOD of SALE. THIS SALE will begin on Tues∣day the 9th. Day of April, 1695▪ exactly at Ten in the Morning: And I do promise that every Book men∣tioned in this Catalogue shall be in the Room where Sold at that time. The Books are to be seen every Day till Saturday before the SALE.
