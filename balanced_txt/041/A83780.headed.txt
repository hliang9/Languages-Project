Die Jovis, 8. Julii, 1646. ORdered by the Commons Assem∣bled in Parliament, That all such Persons as have presented their Petitions at Goldsmiths-Hall, or agreed to their Com∣positions, and shall not come in before the the first of August next, and prosecute their Compositions to effect, shall lose the whole benefit of the favour intended by their Com∣positions, and bee reputed among those that still stand out, and have not rendred themselves to the Parliament. This to bee forthwith Printed and publi∣shed: And that the care hereof bee referred to the Committee at Goldsmiths-Hall. H. Elsynge Cler. Parl. D. Com.
