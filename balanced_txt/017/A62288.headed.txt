Anagram and Acrostick, On His Sacred Majesty, K. JAMES II. Relating to the Late Triple CONJUNCTION OF Saturn & Jupiter in Leo, which began in October 1682, and still continues Anagram, A JAMES STUART. JAM TUA EST ARS. Sir, Now is the Time (if You Judge it meet,) That Whigs and Trimmers Truckle under Feet. AN ACROSTICK. JOVE and Saturn in the Chief Regal Signe, Astonish more than May's Great Twenty Nine: Mighty was CHARLES, much greater JAMES shall be, EXCLUSIVE BILLS can't change Heav'ns firm Decree SIR, at Your Feet These Glorious Planets fall, Striving to Worship God's Great General. These Messengers (who seldom walk abroad,) Ʋouch, and Own You Their Terrestrial GOD: And so let US; For though We Weep for May, Rejoyce We too, since Great OCTOBER Sway. —— Amongst the rest, give leave for Me, To pay what's due to Your SUPREMACY. Being the Humble Address of ROBERT SCAMLER, M.A. &c. Printed by N.T. at the Entrance into Old Spring-garden near Charing-cross. 1685.
