Mrs. JUDITH HULL, Of Boston, in N. E. Daughter of Mr. Edmund Quincey; late Wife of JOHN HÆ²LL Esq deceased. A Diligent, Constant, Fruitfull Reader and Hearer of the Word of GOD, Rested from her Labours, Iune, 22. 1695. being the seventh day of the Week, a little before Sun-set; just about the time She used to begin the Sabbath. Anno Aetatis suae 69. Epitaph GReat Sarah's Faith; joind with Good Hannah's Prayer; For Hearing of the Word, glad Marles Care; Aged Elizabeths Just Walk; To dwell Nigh Prophets, a true Shunamitish Zeal; An Humble Soul, Trim'd with an High Neglect Of Gay Things, but with Ancient Glories deck't; All these Expir'd at once! Array'd with Them, Our HULDAH's gone to Gods Jerusalem, Without a Figure so, with her Last Breath Shee Triumph'd o'er that Holophernes, DEATH.
