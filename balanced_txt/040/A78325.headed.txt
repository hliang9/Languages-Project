A CATALOGVE OF THE NAMES OF the Knights for the Coun∣ties, Citizens, Burgesses for the Boroughes, and Barons for the Ports for the House of Com∣mons, for this Parliament. BEGVN AT Westminster the 3. of November, 1640. The fourth Impression corrected and amended. LONDON, Printed for Thomas Walkley, and are to bee sold at his Shop, at the signe of the flying Horse, betweene Brittains Bursse, and Yorke House 1641.
A Proclamation before the L. STEVVARD. THe Kings most excellent Majesty doth straightly charge and command all manner of Persons chosen for the Knights, Citi∣zens, and Burgesses, to attend in this pre∣sent Parliament, that they, nor any of them doe presume to fit, or take their places in the lower House of Parliament untill they, and every of them have first taken the Oathes of Supremacie, and Allegiance in the usuall manner and place; nor untill the Sheriffe shall make returne of his Writ according to Statute, unto the Clarke of the Crowne in Chancery, and his, or their names bee there entred in such manner as hath hereto∣fore beene accustomed upon paine of the perill shall fall thereon. God save the King. ALl Knights, Citizens, and Burgesses, chosen to at∣tend this present Parliament; make your appea∣rance, and answer to your names as you shall bee called. The names of the Knights, Citizens, and Burgesses of the Counties, Cities, and Borough-Townes of England, and Wales, and the Baronie of the Ports now sitting in Parliament holden at Westminster the 3. day of November, 1640. in the sixteenth yeare of the Raigne of our Soveraigne Lord King Charles.
