Die Sabbati, 24. August. 1644. IT is this day Ordered and Ordained by the Lords and Commons assembled in Parliament, That the Serjeants and Councellors at Law, in the severall Counties of this Kingdom, within the power of Parliament, shall execute the Commis∣sions of Oyer and Terminer, and Goal-delivery, wherein they are nominated Commissioners, notwithstanding any Statute or Clause therein to the contrary. Hen. Elsynge, Cler. Parl. D. Com. Die Sabbati, 24. August. 1644. IT is this day Ordered and Ordained by the Lords and Commons assembled in Parliament, That Commissions of Sewers shall be granted by the Commissioners of the Great Seal, for the seve∣rall Counties of this Kingdom, within the power of the Parliament; although the Lord Treasu∣rer, or Lord Chief Iustices be absent. Hen. Elsynge, Cler. Parl. D. Com. London, Printed for Edward Husbands. 1644.
