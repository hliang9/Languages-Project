VVHereas formerly the Practical Musick performed in this School on the Act Saturday, and other Times, hath been much di∣sturb'd and hinder'd by divers Persons (Men and Women) pressing in within the Rail, set up on pur∣pose that the said Exercise should be performed with the best Advantage, for the Honor of the University. These are therefore to requrie all Person be∣longing to this University, and others, that they do not press in within the said Rail; excepting such as the Musick-Professor shall judge necessary for the better Performance of that Exercise, as they will answer the contrary. Given under my Hand this 6tsh day of July, 1671. P. MEWS Vice-Cancel.
