15. Iune, 1645. IT is desired that all Ministers in Lon∣don, the Liberties, and within the Lines of Communication doe this Sabbath day blesse God that hee hath beene pleased to heare our Prayers in the behalf of our Army; and hath given the Parliament a glorious Victory, killed many, taken many Prisoners, the Standard with the Ramping Lyon in Gold, with the Crown upon it ta∣ken, and Colonell Crumwell pursuing; the Fight began on Saturday the 14. of Iune, a∣bout 12 a clock at noon, and held a terrible fight for about half an houre: and it is re∣ported in the pursuit Prince Rupert is taken. Tho. Atkin Major.
