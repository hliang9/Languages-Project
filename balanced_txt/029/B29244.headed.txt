O what have you done with our Brethren on shore, as they sayled in Barbarie? Wée laid them aboard on the Starboard side, with hey, &c. And we through them into the Sea so wide, and alongst, &c. Such mercy as you have shewed unto them, with hey, &c. Then the like mercy shall you have againe, and alongst, &c. Wée laid them aboard on the Larboard side, with hey, &c. Now gallant Sea-men all adieu, with hey, &c. This is the last newes that I can write to you, to Englands Coast from Barbarie. FINIS. Printed for F. Coles, J. Wright, Tho. Vre, W. Gilbertson.
