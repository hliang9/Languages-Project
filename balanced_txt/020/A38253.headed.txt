Die Iovis, 9 Augusti, 1649. ORdered by the COMMONS as∣sembled in Parliament, That it bee referred to the Committee of Gold∣smiths-hall, to issue Warrants, and take an effectuall Course for Collecting the Arrears of Rents due from, or remain∣ing in the hands of any the Tenants, by vertue of former Orders and Restraints; and cause the same to bee brought into the Treasury at Goldsmiths-hall, and there to be kept in a distinct Treasury, to bee disposed of as the Parliament shall hereafter appoint. Hen. Scobell Cler. Parliament. Printed by Richard Cotes, 1649.
