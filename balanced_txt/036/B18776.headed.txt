A CATALOGUE OF Latin and English BOOKS In QUIRES, to be Sold by AUCTION In Numbers To the Book sellers of London and Westminster only. On Monday the Seventh of this Instant September, 1691. At the Sign of the Bear in Ave-Mary-Lane, near Ludgate-Street, at Three of the Clock in the Afternoon, and so to continue daily till all the BOOKS are Sold. By BENJ. WALFORD. LONDON: Printed in the Year MDCXCI.
CONDITIONS OF This present Sale of BOOKS in Quires to be Sold by AƲCTION, at the Bear in Ave-Mary-Lane, on Monday the Seventh of September, 1691. THat he who bids most is the Buyer, &c. That the Number of each Book proposed to be Sold, shall be mention'd before they are set up. That it shall be at the Liberty of the Exposer to nominate how many of every Book shall be sold at once. That if any Book so sold, upon Collating before Delivery, be found to be Imperfect, an Abatement shall be made proportionably, or the Books returned. That the Books shall be ready to be delivered within Six Days af∣ter the Sale is ended to the respective Buyers, who are obliged to collate their Books, and take the same away, giving Security for the Pay∣ment of the Money to Benjamin Walford, for the Ʋse of the Credi∣tors of Mr. Adiel Mill, upon the Terms following: To such as shall buy to the Value of 25 l. shall be allowed Three Months Credit for the payment thereof. To such as shall buy to the Value of 50 l. shall be allowed Six Months Credit for the payment of the same. Any Person exceeding the first Sum of 50 l. shall be allowed Three Months Credit for the payment of each 50 l. he shall buy more, not exceeding the Sum of Three Hundred Pounds. Any Person making prompt payment, shall have half a Years Re-bate at the rate of Six Pounds per Cent.
