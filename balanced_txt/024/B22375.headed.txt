Die Veneris, 18 November, 1642. IT is this day Ordered by the Com∣mons assembled in Parliament, That the Inhabitants of the seve∣rall Counties shall have power to assemble and gather together the best Force they can, by the directions of the Deputy-Lievtenants, Captains, and other the Officers appointed by Parliament; the which Deputy-Lievtenants, Captains, and other Of∣ficers are hereby required to give their best di∣rections and assistance herein, either by Trained Bands or otherwise, to defend themselves and their Countries from plundering. Hen. Elsynge, Cler. Parl. D. Com.
