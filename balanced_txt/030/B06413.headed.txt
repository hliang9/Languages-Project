In the praise of Typography BLush not to see a Virgin press'd Arts fairest Hand-Maid, though she's dress'd In Sable and in Argent, for Her Coat is Nobler than of Or: Black makes her Beauty spots, and White Concenters to compleat Delight: Yet know unto her Dowry's due Something for the Interview: And if your Liberall looks commend Her feature, she'l remain Your Freind, Not unto Death, for know that She Can give you Immortality. Fo Vaux.
